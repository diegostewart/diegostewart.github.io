## About Me

I'm Diego Stewart — a Data Architect and SaaS builder based in Spain.

I work at the intersection of data engineering, system design, and product thinking. 
Most of my work revolves around designing scalable data platforms, structuring governance correctly, and making complex systems understandable and usable.

---

## What I Write About

On this blog, I write about:

- Data architecture decisions and trade-offs  
- Databricks and modern data platforms  
- Designing multi-tenant SaaS systems  
- Technical leadership and system thinking  
- Lessons learned from building real products  

Writing helps me clarify my thinking. Publishing helps me refine it.

## How I Think About Systems

I care about:

- Simplicity over cleverness  
- Explicit design over accidental complexity  
- Ownership and long-term maintainability  
- Business impact, not just technical elegance  

Technology is only valuable if it solves real problems.
